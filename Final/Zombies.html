Welcome to Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 Zombies

In this heart-pounding experience, you find yourself trapped in a series of nightmarish scenarios, battling hordes of the undead in an endless struggle for survival. As you fight for your life, you'll encounter a variety of eerie environments ranging from abandoned research facilities to haunted castles, each teeming with their own brand of terror.

To aid you in your fight against the relentless undead, you'll have access to a range of powerful weapons, from traditional firearms to experimental energy weapons. But your arsenal isn't limited to just guns; you'll also have access to a selection of mysterious artifacts and powerful magic spells, each with their own unique abilities to turn the tide of battle in your favor.

But perhaps your greatest ally in this fight is the Perk-a-Cola machines scattered throughout the map. These vending machines offer a variety of potent elixirs that grant you temporary enhancements, from increased speed and firepower to faster health regeneration and even the ability to revive fallen teammates with a single touch.

Juggernog: Price: 2500: Increses health and durability.

Speed Cola: Price: 3000: Reload faster.

Double Tap Root Beer: Price 2000: Doubles fire rate and doubles damage.

Quick Revive(solo): Price 500: Revive yourself when downed.

Quick Revive(co-op): Price 1500: Speed up the revival process for fallen teammates.

Electric Cherry: Price 2000: When reloading this perk electrifies all enimies nearby.

Mule Kick: Price 4000: Allows player to have a third weapon.

Stanima up: Price 2000: Increases stanima.

Deadshot Daiquiri: Price 1500: Increases hipfire accuracy and auto aims to Zombies heads when aiming down sights.

Widows Wine: Price 4000: Replaces grenades with Widow's Wine grenades and melee attacks to the player will cause a small explosion that will wrap the attacker in webs.
